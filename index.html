About

About Me

Hi, I'm Stephen Singler - a game designer focused on creating engaging, player-centered experiences through systems, level, and narrative design. I'm especially interested in how mechanics, structure, and player choice work together to create meaningful play.

My background includes both digital game development and a lifelong connection to video games, alongside years of experience playing and running tabletop roleplaying games. Together, those experiences have shaped how I think about design, iteration, and player experience. They've given me a strong appreciation for systems design, emergent gameplay, player agency, and the ways people engage with rules, space, and story.

I'm currently finishing my master's in game design and looking to bring a thoughtful, adaptable design mindset to the games industry through roles in game design, systems design, level design, combat design, QA, or any other adjacent development work.
