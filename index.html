About Me

Hi! I'm Stephen Singler, an aspiring game designer passionate about crafting engaging and immersive experiences in both video games and tabletop roleplaying games (TTRPGs). My design interests span combat systems, level creation, and narrative design. I have extensive experience with TTRPGs like Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition and Pathfinder 2nd Edition, where I love spending time theorycrafting characters and exploring gameplay mechanics. With a lifelong love for video games and a deep appreciation for the unique storytelling of TTRPGs, I'm eager to bring my creativity, dedication, and fresh perspective to game design.
