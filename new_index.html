About

Designer first, adaptable across roles

I’m currently finishing my master’s in game design and building toward a career in the games industry. My strongest interests are game design, systems design, narrative design, and level design, but I’m also comfortable contributing in technical design, implementation, QA, and research-oriented roles.

My work tends to focus on how mechanics communicate meaning: how player choice, level structure, systems interactions, and feedback loops can create both compelling gameplay and stronger narrative payoff.

I enjoy projects that sit at the intersection of design thinking, iteration, and implementation — especially when they involve prototyping, player experience tuning, or translating abstract ideas into playable systems.
